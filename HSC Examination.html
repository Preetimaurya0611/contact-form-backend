Instructions for Examination Registration
  • To apply for Examination, you must verify using OTP.
  • Enter RMN (Registered Mobile Number) or RE (Registered Email).
  • Click on the [Send OTP] button.
  • If your mobile number is not registered, contact the institute for assistance.
  • (Only for students registered from academic year 2020–21 onwards)
  • System will generate a unique OTP (One Time Password).
  • Enter the received OTP and click on [Verify OTP].
  • After verification, the HSC Examination Form will be displayed.
  • Fill all required details and pay the corresponding examination fee.
